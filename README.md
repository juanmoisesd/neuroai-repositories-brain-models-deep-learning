## Description

This dataset provides a curated and structured inventory of the main repositories, databases, simulation frameworks, toolkits, and research projects at the intersection of computational neuroscience and artificial intelligence (Neuro-AI). It covers resources relating biological neural networks with artificial neural networks, deep learning architectures, brain models, and neural representations.

Includes 18 entries compiled from Perplexity AI (February 2026), organized into categories: model repositories, evaluation benchmarks, neuromorphic datasets, brain atlases, data portals, simulation frameworks, analysis toolkits, and large-scale research projects.
